# UGC Incentive Program

## Overview

The app has a built-in viral loop: shareable analysis images with skeleton overlay, grade badge, and "Analyzed by Tennis IQ" watermark. This program incentivizes users to actually share.

---

## Share Feature (Built Into App)

The Share button in the analysis results screen generates a 1080x1920 image containing:
- Skeleton overlay of the user's body
- Letter grade badge (A through F)
- Stroke type label (Forehand, Backhand, Serve, Volley)
- "Analyzed by Tennis IQ" watermark at the bottom

Users can share to Instagram Stories, TikTok, save to camera roll, or send via messages.

---

## Incentive Tiers

### Tier 1: Share for Free Month
**Offer:** Post your AI analysis on Instagram or TikTok with #TennisIQ and tag @tennisiq. DM us the link for 1 free month of premium.

**Verification:**
- User DMs screenshot of their post with #TennisIQ
- Post must be public
- Post must include the analysis image from the app (watermark visible)
- Grant promo code for 1 month free

**Promo Code System:**
- Create promo codes in App Store Connect (Subscription Offers)
- Generate batch of 100 codes at a time
- Track: code -> user -> post link

### Tier 2: Monthly Challenge
**Offer:** Every month, the user who shows the biggest improvement (using progress tracking screenshots) wins 3 months free + featured on the official account.

**How to run:**
1. Announce on social: "Show us your biggest improvement this month"
2. Users share before/after screenshots from the progress dashboard
3. Pick the winner based on grade improvement
4. Feature their story on the official account
5. Send 3-month promo code

### Tier 3: Creator Program (Month 2+)
**For users who consistently create content:**
- Free lifetime premium access
- Early access to new features
- Name listed as "Community Coach" in the app
- Monthly "Creator Spotlight" on official channels
- Potential affiliate commission (30% of referral revenue)

**Criteria:**
- Posted 5+ analysis videos with #TennisIQ
- Generated 10+ App Store installs (tracked via their promo code)
- Active user of the app (uses it at least weekly)
