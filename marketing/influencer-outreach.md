# Influencer & Borrowed Channel Outreach

## Strategy

Focus on mid-tier tennis content creators (10K-100K followers) who already do stroke analysis or tennis instruction content. The app is a natural complement to their existing content. Offer free lifetime premium access — no upfront payment. Let the product sell itself.

---

## YouTube Targets

### Tier 1: Tennis Coaching Channels (10K-100K subs)
These channels regularly post stroke analysis videos — the app is a direct content upgrade for them.

**Search for channels with these keywords:**
- "tennis forehand analysis"
- "tennis serve breakdown"
- "tennis lesson"
- "tennis tips"
- "recreational tennis"

**What to look for:**
- Regular upload schedule (at least weekly)
- Engagement in comments (active community)
- Content focused on technique, not just match highlights
- Average view count > 5K

### Outreach Template (YouTube)
```
Subject: AI Tennis Analysis App — Free Lifetime Access for Your Channel

Hi [Channel Name],

I watch your stroke analysis videos regularly and built something I 
think you'll find interesting — Tennis IQ, an iOS app that uses 
computer vision (Apple Vision) and GPT-4o to analyze tennis strokes 
with skeleton overlays and letter grades.

I'd love to offer you free lifetime premium access. No strings attached.

Why I think you'd like it:
- It shows a full skeleton overlay on video playback
- Grades each stroke A through F with detailed mechanics breakdown
- Could be a cool complement to your analysis videos
- Your audience would probably love seeing AI vs. human coach comparisons

If you try it and find it useful, I'd be happy to set up an affiliate 
link so you earn from any downloads you drive.

Here's the App Store link: [link]

Either way, keep making great tennis content. 🎾

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Instagram Targets

### Tier 1: Tennis Content Creators (5K-50K followers)
Look for accounts that post Reels about tennis technique, drills, and improvement.

**Search hashtags:**
- #tenniscoach
- #tennistips
- #tennisdrills
- #tennispro
- #tennisinstruction

**What to look for:**
- Regular Reels (3+ per week)
- High engagement rate (likes/comments relative to followers)
- Content about technique and improvement (not just match clips)
- They actually play tennis (not just aggregator accounts)

### Outreach Template (Instagram DM)
```
Hey [Name]! Love your tennis content, especially the [specific post] video.

I built an AI tennis coaching app (Tennis IQ) that does skeleton 
overlay analysis and grades strokes A through F. Would love to give you 
free lifetime premium access to try it.

No ask — just think it could make for some cool content if you dig it. 
Let me know and I'll set you up!
```

## Affiliate Program Structure

Once a creator tries the app and likes it, offer:
- **Commission**: 30% of first-year revenue from their referral link
- **Tracking**: Use unique promo codes or App Store campaign links
- **Payout**: Monthly via PayPal or direct deposit
- **Minimum**: No minimum payout threshold for first 6 months

### How to Track
- Create unique App Store campaign URLs for each creator
- Use promo codes tied to creator names (e.g., code "TENNISTOM" for free month)

### Follow-up Schedule
- Day 0: Initial outreach
- Day 5: Follow up if no response (1 time only)
- Day 14: If they tried it, ask for feedback
- Day 30: Check if they posted content, offer affiliate setup

### Response Rate Expectations
- Cold DM: 5-15% response rate
- Email: 10-20% response rate
- Of responders, ~50% will try the app
- Of those who try, ~20-30% will post content organically
