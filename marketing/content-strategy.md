# UGC-First Content Strategy — Tennis IQ

## Core Thesis

The app's "magic moment" — skeleton overlay + instant AI grades — is inherently visual and shareable. Every piece of content should showcase this moment to create a "how is this real?" reaction that drives downloads.

---

## 4 Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: "Watch AI Analyze This" (40% of content)
**Format:** 15-30 sec Reels/TikToks
**Why:** This is the demo that sells. Pure wow factor.

**Script Template:**
```
[0-3s HOOK]: "I let AI analyze my [stroke type]..."
[3-8s SETUP]: Show yourself hitting the stroke naturally
[8-15s REVEAL]: Cut to the app showing skeleton overlay
[15-22s PAYOFF]: Show the grade + key coaching feedback
[22-30s CTA]: "Download Tennis IQ — link in bio"
```

**Variations:**
- "AI rates my serve" (focus on one stroke)
- "Every stroke I hit today, graded by AI"
- "Side by side: what I see vs. what AI sees"
- "AI found a problem I never noticed"
- "Pro player form vs. mine — AI comparison"

### Pillar 2: "Tennis Tips from AI" (25% of content)
**Format:** 30-60 sec educational Reels
**Why:** Provides value, establishes authority, attracts tennis learners

**Script Template:**
```
[0-3s HOOK]: "Your [stroke] is stuck at a [grade] because of this..."
[3-10s PROBLEM]: Show the common mistake with skeleton overlay
[10-25s EXPLANATION]: AI coaching cue explaining what's wrong
[25-40s FIX]: Show the correct form with overlay
[40-50s RESULT]: Show improved grade
[50-60s CTA]: "Get your own AI analysis — link in bio"
```

**Content Ideas:**
- "3 things AI noticed about my serve toss"
- "Why your forehand follow-through kills your power"
- "The backhand mistake that causes elbow pain"
- "AI says 80% of recreational players do this wrong"
- "Fix your contact point in 5 minutes"

### Pillar 3: "Before vs. After" (20% of content)
**Format:** 15-45 sec transformation Reels
**Why:** Social proof. Shows the app works.

**Script Template:**
```
[0-3s HOOK]: "Day 1 vs. Day [X] with AI coaching..."
[3-10s BEFORE]: Show Day 1 stroke with skeleton + grade
[10-20s JOURNEY]: Quick montage of practicing
[20-30s AFTER]: Show current stroke with skeleton + improved grade
[30-40s STATS]: Show progress dashboard improvement
[40-45s CTA]: "Start your improvement journey — link in bio"
```

**Series Ideas:**
- "30 days of AI coaching" (ongoing series)
- "Can I get an A on every stroke in 60 days?"
- "Weekly improvement check-in"
- "From D to B+ on my serve"

### Pillar 4: "Rate My Stroke" (15% of content)
**Format:** Interactive engagement posts + stories
**Why:** Drives DMs, creates user submissions, builds community

**Script Template:**
```
[0-3s HOOK]: "You sent me your serve. Let's see what AI thinks..."
[3-8s SHOW]: Play the submitted clip
[8-18s ANALYZE]: Show AI analysis result
[18-25s FEEDBACK]: Read the coaching cue
[25-30s CTA]: "DM me YOUR stroke to get analyzed"
```

**Engagement Tactics:**
- Instagram Stories polls: "What grade do you think this serve gets?"
- "Comment your tennis level, I'll tell you what AI would find"
- "Rate this form 1-10 before AI reveals the grade"
- User spotlight: Feature best submissions weekly

## Video Production Tips

1. **Film in landscape for tennis, post in portrait for social** — use cropping
2. **Always show the app UI** — the skeleton overlay IS the content
3. **Captions are mandatory** — 85% watch without sound
4. **First 3 seconds decide everything** — lead with the hook, not a logo
5. **Natural lighting on court** — no indoor desk demos
6. **Show real imperfections** — authenticity > perfection
7. **End every video with a clear CTA** — "Link in bio" or "Download free"

## Reposting Strategy

- Best TikToks -> Instagram Reels (same day or next day)
- Best Reels -> YouTube Shorts (1-2 day delay)
- Best performers -> Re-edit and repost after 2-3 weeks
- Top 3 monthly performers -> Pin to profile / add to highlights
