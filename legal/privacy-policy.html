Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

Tennis IQ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple user identifier and, optionally, your name. We do not receive or store your Apple ID email unless you choose to share it.

Video and Pose Data: When you record a tennis session, the video is processed on your device using Apple Vision framework for pose estimation. The extracted pose data (joint coordinates, not video) is sent to our servers for AI coaching analysis. Your raw video files remain on your device and are never uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly share them.

Usage Data: We collect anonymized usage analytics including app opens, features used, and session counts to improve the app experience. This data is not linked to your personal identity.

Feedback: If you submit in-app feedback, we store your rating, comments, and basic device information (device model, iOS version) to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered stroke analysis and coaching feedback
  • To track your progress over time within the app
  • To process your subscription and manage your account
  • To improve the quality of our AI coaching analysis
  • To respond to your feedback and support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS) with row-level security policies. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted via TLS. Pose data is processed in memory on our servers and not permanently stored beyond your session records.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple Sign In: Authentication provider (Apple's privacy policy applies)
  • OpenAI: Processes pose data for coaching analysis (OpenAI's usage policy applies; no personal data is sent)
  • Supabase: Database and authentication infrastructure

5. Data Retention

We retain your session data and analysis results for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at support@tennisiq.com. Video files are stored only on your device and are managed by you.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email.
