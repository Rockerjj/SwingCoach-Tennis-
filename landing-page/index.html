How It Works

Three steps to better tennis

1

Film Your Game

Prop your phone courtside. Hit record. Play naturally — forehands, backhands, serves, volleys. Up to 30 minutes per session.

2

AI Watches Every Swing

Apple Vision tracks your body in real time. AI evaluates your mechanics and tells you what a real coach would say — in plain English.

3

Know What to Practice

Get one clear coaching cue per stroke, a visual showing the fix, and a specific drill with reps. Take it to the court tomorrow.
