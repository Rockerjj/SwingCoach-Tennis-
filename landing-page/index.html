How It Works

Three steps to better tennis

1

Film Your Game

Prop your phone courtside. Hit record. Play naturally — forehands, backhands, serves, volleys. Up to 30 minutes per session.

2

AI Watches Every Swing

Apple Vision tracks your body in real time. GPT-5.4 evaluates your mechanics and tells you what a real coach would say — in plain English.

3

Know What to Practice

Get one clear coaching cue per stroke type, a visual showing the fix, and a specific drill with reps. Take it to the court tomorrow.

What You Get

A real coach, not a data dump

Tennique tells you what to fix in plain coaching language — not confusing angle measurements.

🎯

One Clear Fix Per Stroke

"Get your racket back earlier" — not "elbow angle is 105° vs ideal 90-120°." You get the one thing that matters most.

🦴

See the Fix, Not Just Hear It

Your skeleton overlay morphs from your current form to the ideal position. Watch your arm straighten, your hips open, your finish rise.

🗣️

Live Coaching While You Play

Turn on live mode and hear coaching cues spoken aloud during recording. "Extend through the ball!" — right when you need it.

⭐

Your Best Swing Highlighted

It's not all criticism. Tennique shows you what you're doing right too — so you can repeat it.

📋

Drills You'll Actually Do

Every fix comes with a specific drill, rep count, and a YouTube demo video. Take it to the court, not just your notes app.

📈

Track Your Progress

See your scores improve over time. Know which strokes are getting better and which still need work.
